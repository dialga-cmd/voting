The Voting Process

Follow these simple steps to cast your vote securely

1

Account Creation

Register Your Account

Create a secure account using your email address and set up a strong password. You'll need to verify your identity and eligibility to vote.

  • Provide valid email address
  • Create strong password (8+ characters)
  • Verify email through confirmation link
  • Complete identity verification process
2

Login & Authentication

Secure Login Process

Access your account using multi-factor authentication to ensure maximum security. Your identity is verified before granting access to voting.

  • Enter email and password
  • Complete two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Biometric verification (optional)
  • Access granted to eligible elections
3

Browse Elections

View Available Elections

Browse through all active elections you're eligible to participate in. Each election displays detailed information about candidates and voting deadlines.

  • View election titles and descriptions
  • Check voting start and end dates
  • See candidate profiles and manifestos
  • Review your voting eligibility status
4

Review Candidates

Research Your Options

Make informed decisions by reviewing detailed candidate information, their policies, background, and qualifications before casting your vote.

  • Read candidate biographies and credentials
  • Review policy positions and manifestos
  • View candidate photos and contact information
  • Access additional campaign materials
5

Cast Your Vote

Secure Voting Process

Select your preferred candidate and submit your vote. The system uses advanced encryption to ensure your vote is secure and anonymous.

  • Select your preferred candidate
  • Review your choice before submission
  • Confirm vote with final authentication
  • Receive encrypted vote confirmation
6

View Results

Transparent Results

Once voting closes, view real-time election results with detailed analytics. All results are transparent and verifiable through our audit system.

  • Access results immediately after voting closes
  • View detailed vote counts and percentages
  • Download official result certificates
  • Access voting audit trail for verification
